Hi, I'm Nathan Nadler-Nir
a designer, filmmaker, and creative technologist building digital gardens.

I work across media, from video, illustration and 3D to UX/UI and Web. Whether I'm shaping a brand, producing a documentary, or sketching out a product, I always put people at the center of the process.

Over the years, I've:
🎬 Produced mountains of film and video content with companies, collaborators & arts festivals
🚀 Led the creative strategy of tech startups like Momint and Dado
💻 Developed custom web experiences for international clients
🧠 Specialized in creative execution, story structure, and productivity systems (I love Notion)

My practice balances highly efficient problem solving with the joy of experimentation, it's grounded in collaboration, fuelled by curiosity, and informed by a lifelong fascination with digital tools.
